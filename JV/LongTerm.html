The Simulation

Apt’s Trading Simulation Platform allows users with varying levels of experience in energy trading to enhance their knowledge of UK trading concepts.

Contents

​​The game simulates a trading situation where the players are in charge of a 420 MW Coal fired Power Station with the objective to make the highest profit while making sure the correct hedging strategies are used.

What Participants Will Learn

Wholesale market for electricity trading. Core electricity trading products. The reason for electricity trading and hedging.

Game Structure

Trading a one-year period. Split into calendar quarters. Settlement report at the end of each quarter. Three legs: power, coal and carbon (trading the Clean Dark Spread).Find profitable opportunity and sell the power.

Generation, Counterparty and Market Risk

The players will have to manage generation, counterparty and market price risk and  ensure the power station has the require coal stocks and carbon balance.

Profit

Credit limits and credit risk, arbitrage opportunities, find profitable opportunity and sell the power.

Real-Time Issues

The user can also be subjected to real time issues. This could be plant issues resulting in a restricted load, a full or partial plant trip or an IT system failure.

Profit/Loss

A real time profit and loss updates with every action taken.

Credit

The impact of credit constraints on hedging activities. Credit limits are set between counterparties and are reviewed on an ongoing process throughout the simulation.
